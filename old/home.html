Welcome!

Letterpress is the craft of using century-old cast iron printing equipment to press hand set lead type into paper to create custom prints. This old-fashioned printing method is in the middle of a huge revival that is sweeping through the stationary world. Cephalopod Press and her owner, Adriene Woodworth, are proud to be a part of the movement.

Cephalopod Press designs and produces greeting cards, postcards, small posters and other hand-made editioned prints using hand-set typography, hand-carved printing blocks, woodcut, and other methods to create unique gifts and keepsakes.
